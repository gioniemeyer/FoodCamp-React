## About

Do you remember the first FoodCamp? If not, please, let me introduce you: https://github.com/gioniemeyer/FoodCamp

## But, this one has new features!

- Now you can choose more than one main course and in more qty

- Now you can choose more than one dessert and in more qty

- Now you can choose more than one drink and in more qty

- After choose at least one of each category, you may finish your order!

- The food lover will be redirected to wpp.

## Future Features

- It should appear a suggestion text order to be sent to wpp when finish order

- A confirmation message may appear before the food lover gets redirected to wpp. You know, in case your hungry gets bigger than you wallet...
